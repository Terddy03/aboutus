Our Lady Of Fatima University

About Us

Our Lady of Fatima University is a Higher Education Institution in the Philippines. OLFU, established in 1967, is one of the respected and admired educational institutions in the country today particularly in the field of paramedical programs such as Medicine, Dentistry, Nursing, Medical Laboratory Science, Physical and Respiratory Therapy, and Pharmacy among others, together with non-paramedical programs such as Hotel and Restaurant Management, Travel Management, Computer Studies, Maritime Education, Criminology, Business and Accountancy, and Education, to mention a few. OLFU also offers different programs under its Basic Education, Senior High School, and Graduate School. The university is currently present in six campuses: Valenzuela City, Quezon City, Antipolo, Pampanga, Nueva Ecija, and Laguna.
